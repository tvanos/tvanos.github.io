GISC9312-D1: ArcGIS 3D Analysis
Niagara College

This project serves to present the findings and final map set of the spatial analysis undertaken for the Re-introduction of the Finlay’s Fabulous Fictitious Fern (Osmunda Finlayensis). Niagara College’s horticultural group, along with the environmental restoration group had the foresight to collect specimens of the fern for propagation and eventually re-introduce the species into its natural habitat. A spatial analysis is undertaken to determine the ideal placement for introduction. The spatial analysis was completed as a Multi-criteria Evaluation (MCE) of four quintessential factors determining the growth of the fern species. The criteria is as follows: Slope, Aspect, Hill shade and Soil composition.

Highlights include:

  • ArcMap 10.3
  • ArcScene
  • MCE
  • Fuzzy Overlay
  • Hillshade and Slope Analysis
  • 3D Visualization
